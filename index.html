One-day Website Redesigns

The challenge was simple. One day, three website redesigns. In this excersise, I took on a variety of subjects, and found ways to give them a cleaner, more modern, and inviting design.

Target Home Page

Target is known for their clean and modern asthetics. However, their current home page feels cluttered and busy. Staying within their brand guidelines, I added clarity by simplifying what was displayed and gave focus rather than trying to draw attention to too many things at once.
